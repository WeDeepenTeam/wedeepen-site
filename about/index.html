The Story

How WeDeepen Began

It started in 2014, in New York City. Christina was hosting events called Underground Unattached — gathering smart, accomplished, attractive single people in one room, believing that proximity plus quality would lead to love.

For two years she watched. And what she saw wasn't working the way she thought it would. The right people were in the room. But the room wasn't enough.

Pressure. Expectations. The nervous energy people walked in with. You can't event your way past that — you have to be trained for it.

Then a second signal arrived — this one from the other side of the room. While Christina was pouring everything into the dating scene, the couples kept showing up asking the same thing: don’t forget about us.

The only things being marketed to relationships were therapy and sex parties — pathology on one end, chaos on the other, and nothing in between. No education. No ongoing community. No place to keep growing together once the wedding photos went up. They wanted tools. They wanted a cohort. They wanted somewhere to practice.

That recognition sent her down a rabbit hole that changed everything. She discovered an entire industry dedicated to love as a learnable skill — tools, teachers, frameworks, communication, conflict resolution, typologies, somatics, tantra, human design, erotic blueprints. Disciplines most of her friends had never heard of, let alone practiced.

She went deep. Got trained. Studied with the best. And then asked the obvious question:

Why isn’t there one place that brings all of this together — so people don’t have to stumble through forty teachers to figure out what actually works?

That question became WeDeepen.

Today, WeDeepen is the first curated ecosystem for relationship education. A members-only community (Love Club), a 3-day immersive retreat series (Love Immersion), a rotating council of world-class Love Strategists, and a growing network of in-person experiences — starting in Los Angeles, now rooted in Austin.

From the events we create — The Dating Dojo, Playful Portal, and Shop & Shag at Erewhon — to the ones we curate and collaborate on: KASHF, Unleash!, MindTravel, The Magic of Human Connection, Mystery Temple, and Beyond Biohacking Conference.

Thousands have come through. The work is intimate, intelligent, and permission-granting. It's not self-help. It's love training.

Our Values

WeDeepen with 🌱 GUIDANCE

Eight values that shape how we show up — with each other, with our members, and with ourselves.

Growth comes first

You believe in feeling a sense of purpose, and that purpose is growth. Individual growth. Collective growth. The kind of growth that makes the world a better place.

Unoffendable

Anger is extraordinarily easy. It's our default setting. Love is challenging. Love is a miracle. You grow in love.

Intuitive flow

When operating from the soul level, you flow.

Desire to receive for the sake of sharing

There is no enjoying the possession of anything valuable unless you have ones to share it with. You give to enjoy the shared life.

Accepts change

Progress is impossible without change, and those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything. You can change.

Nurture, self-discovery & freewill

Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom. You trust your life journey and the lessons it's teaching you. No one will care for you better than you.

Curiosity & thoughtful discourse

It is not our differences that divide us. It is our inability to recognize, accept and celebrate those differences. You lead with curiosity.

Essentially, our wholeness depends on relationships

The quality of my life is determined by the quality of my relationships. You love thy neighbor.
