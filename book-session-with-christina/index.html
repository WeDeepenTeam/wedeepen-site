The Framework

The Four Pillars of a Conscious Relationship

My mentorship is rooted in the WeDeepen framework. These principles guide every session — because the quality of your relationships mirrors the depth of your relationship with yourself.

I.

No Attachment to the Outcome — Growth Comes First

Love is a byproduct of becoming. When growth leads, connection follows.

II.

Own Your Sh*t

Radical ownership of your patterns, projections, and part to play. The gateway to real intimacy.

III.

All Feelings Are Welcome, No Internal Process Is Condemned

You don’t have to perform wellness here. Every feeling is information; every feeling belongs.

IV.

Practice Love

Love is a skill, not a feeling. We practice it — in language, presence, and embodied action.

Your Mentor

About Christina Weber

I’m Christina Weber — founder of WeDeepen and the Love Club, and forever a student of love. I began this work in 2014 in New York City, hosting Underground Unattached, an invite-only dating event I believed would “solve” connection by putting quality people in the same room. Two years in, I realized proximity wasn’t the point — skill was. That discovery led me into the intimacy industry and ultimately to build WeDeepen: a platform for people who want to master love the way they’ve mastered everything else.

Since then, I’ve curated experiences and teachings through Love Club, Love Immersion, Biohacking Love (co-created with Dave Asprey), The Dating Dojo (co-led with Ken Page, LCSW), RelateFest, and Shop & Shag — helping thousands build the skills and presence real intimacy requires. I also founded Feminine Weapon, a philanthropic initiative that has raised over $100K for survivors of abuse and trafficking.

I host the Deepen with Christina podcast (since 2020), and my work has been featured on Real Time with Bill Maher, Peacock’s Ex-Rated (with Andy Cohen), the Biohacking Conference, the Omega Institute, LA Magazine, Good Day LA, and Wonderland. I’ve studied with many of the world’s most respected relationship teachers — Gay & Katie Hendricks, Annie Lalla, Ken Page, John Gray, Alison Armstrong, Dr. Pat Allen, and Kimi Inch.

Originally from Annapolis · previously NYC & LA · currently based in Austin, TX.
