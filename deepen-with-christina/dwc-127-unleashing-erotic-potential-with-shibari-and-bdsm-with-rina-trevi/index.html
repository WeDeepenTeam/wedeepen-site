Show Notes

In this captivating episode, host Christina Weber dives into the erotic and transformative world of Shibari and BDSM with expert Rina Trevi, a certified professional coach and a leader in awakening erotic potential through Shibari and BDSM practices.

Christina and Rina explore the profound impacts of these practices on personal and relational dynamics. Rina shares her journey from growing up in Russia to becoming a professional dominatrix in New York City, highlighting the freedom and authenticity she discovered through this path. They discuss the psychological and emotional depths of BDSM, emphasizing the importance of safety, vulnerability, and the exploration of desires beyond societal norms.

Listeners will learn about the transformative power of Shibari, not just as an art form but as a means to achieve deep bodily and mental relaxation, trust, and connection. Rina's insights reveal how these practices can help individuals and couples break free from mental constraints, deepen their intimacy, and experience a heightened sense of aliveness.

The role of safety and consent in creating a trusting environment for erotic exploration. How Shibari can facilitate a unique state of surrender and presence.
