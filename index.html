Of everything we’ve studied and taught, this is what we come back to.
Four pillars that keep love alive, honest, and transformative—in yourself, in romance, friendship, business, and beyond.

1

Prioritize Growth

No attachment to outcome. Growth comes first.

2

Own Your Own Sh*t

Take responsibility for your wounds and reactions.

3

All Feelings Are Welcome

No internal process is condemned.

4

Practice Love

In every moment, with every person.

Love is a lifelong practice of shedding ego, armor, and control — combined with ever-increasing acts of sharing. These pillars make that possible.
