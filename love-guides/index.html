The Council

Meet the Love Strategists.

A hand-picked council of world-class teachers, coaches, and practitioners. Each with a distinct specialty. Each extraordinary in their own right. Together, they teach inside the Love Club and at every Love Immersion.

"There is no one teacher. No one modality. No one path. We bring together the world's most extraordinary love and relationship guides so you can explore, find your fit, and go deeper."

Love Strategist & Founder

Christina Weber

Love Industry Pioneer · Speaker · Experience Designer · Coach

Founder of WeDeepen. Christina began her career hosting Underground Unattached dating events in NYC in 2014 — and after recognizing that proximity alone couldn't teach people to love well, she went deep into the underground world of relationship education. Over a decade later, she's curated the most potent experiences in the love industry into one ecosystem.

ICF Certified Coach · iPEC Trained · Heart-Coaching Certified. Based in Austin, TX.

@christinaweber christinalweber.com
Radical Play & Polarity

Jordan Bowditch

Jordan teaches the embodied mechanics of polarity — the magnetic tension between masculine and feminine energy that keeps long-term relationships alive. His work invites radical play, presence, and the kind of permission most people have never been given to desire fully.

Expect somatic work, direct coaching, and a rare fusion of warmth and precision that transforms how people experience their own aliveness.

@jordanbowditch
Deep Transformation & Clarity

Ariana Hall

Ariana works in the precise territory between truth and tenderness. Her coaching cuts through the stories people have been telling themselves for years — and holds them steady while they feel what was underneath.

Known for clarity without cruelty, Ariana is the strategist you go to when you're ready to stop dressing up the same pattern in new clothes.

@arianahall arianahall.com
Biohacking & Longevity

Dave Asprey

The founding father of biohacking — founder of Bulletproof, host of The Human Upgrade podcast, and author of multiple New York Times bestsellers on optimizing the body, brain, and nervous system.

Inside WeDeepen, Dave brings the biology of love to the table: how your nervous system, sleep, and stress physiology directly shape your capacity for intimacy, desire, and sustained partnership.

@dave.asprey daveasprey.com
Cartographer of Love

Annie Lalla

Philosopher, relationship coach, and self-described Cartographer of Love — Annie has spent decades mapping the architecture of great relationships. Her frameworks for conflict, commitment, and communication are used by some of the world's most successful couples.

Expect intelligence, warmth, and a linguistic precision that reorganizes how you understand your own love life.

@annielalla annielalla.com
Intimacy & Embodied Partnership

Yossef Sagi

Yossef brings the ancient and the anatomical together — teaching intimacy as a practice of presence, nervous system regulation, and deep somatic attunement. His work guides partners back to each other at the level of the body, not just the mind.

Known for creating containers where people feel profoundly safe enough to drop the armor they didn't even know they were wearing.

@yossefsagi yossefsagi.com
Feminine Embodiment

Dana Arielle

Dana's work lives at the intersection of feminine embodiment, pleasure, and devotion. She teaches women (and anyone returning to their feminine) how to move from performance into presence, from ambition into receptivity, without losing edge.

A practitioner of sacred movement, erotic self-awareness, and nervous system softening — her presence alone is the transmission.

danaarielle.com
