About the Show

Conversations that go deeper.

Most relationship content stays on the surface — tips, hacks, hot takes. Deepen with Christina goes under it. What actually makes love last? What shuts people down? What does it take to stay curious across decades?

Christina sits down with the Love Strategists, thought leaders, scientists, and practitioners who are reshaping how we understand intimacy — and with the real people living those truths. Unfiltered, intelligent, and always returning to what matters.
